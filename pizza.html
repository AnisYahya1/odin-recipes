Pizza

Description

Get the dough and spread it. Once fully spread, get the sauce and evenly spread across dough. After, place cheese on top and place in oven for 5 minutes then take it out and let it rest. Then YOU CAN EAT

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Cheese
  • Pizza

Steps

  1. Get Dough
  2. Place sauce
  3. Place Cheese
  4. Cook
